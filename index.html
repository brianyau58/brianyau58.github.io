About Me




I am always interested in multimedia, something like graphic design or animation. As I think they are a great medium to express someone's thoughts. I can combine different elements of media like color, image, video, sound to deliver messages, but not just use words. It is way more interesting and able to give a strong impression to the target audience if we use these different elements wisely.

In this Portfolio, some of my works will be displayed. They included logo re-design, some poster ideas, and videos. There are all from different aspects of media and I would like to continue to explore more about the elements of media.
